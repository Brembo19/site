How does blocking work?

We identify malicious IP addresses and domains through advanced scanning and monitoring tools. Once detected, we report them to platforms like AbuseIPDB and collaborate with hosting providers or authorities to restrict access, ensuring a safer digital environment.

What is AbuseIPDB?

AbuseIPDB is a community-driven platform where webmasters and system administrators report IP addresses involved in malicious activities, such as hacking, phishing, or spamming. We contribute to this database to help maintain a global blacklist, protecting networks worldwide.
